The miata's quick summary

I purchased this car in July 2022 from a man moving out of the state to return to his hometown. The car was heavily modified with suspension, wheels, a supercharger and some other minor things done. It was a pretty good price as well. I drove a few hours with one of my friends to pick it up and brough it back home with almost no issues. Throughout my ownership I have done numerous amounts of maintenance to the car, including replacing a clutch, engine and transmission, which are the most difficult and expensive things to replace on most cars. I also removed the supercharger, which was a shame as it was one of the coolest parts of the car. Just this past spring I put on some new body panels and finished replacing the motor. Cars are quite the expensive hobby but nothing feels as rewarding as it.
