About me

My name is Julian Ortega, and I am a senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill studying Political Science and Data Science. I love being outdoors, working on cars, playing video games and learning new things. I am from High Point, North Carolina and am a second generation Salvadoran American.

Working on cars is one of my favorite hobbies because it challenges me both physically and intellectually. Moving big heavy parts and being outside for hours, under a car can be very physically demanding, as well as the problem solving involved with some jobs and diagnosis work.
